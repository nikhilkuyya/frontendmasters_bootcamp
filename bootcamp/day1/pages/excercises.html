Excercises

Physiotherapy Excercise

  • Fingers Movement.

    Press and Hold the fingers down for 5/10/15 sec.

  • Normal Knee Press

    Press with knee and No help from Heap.Hold the Knee down for 5/10/15 sec.

  • Roll Knee Press

    Press with knee and No help from Heap.Hold the Knee down for 5/10/15 sec.

  • Lift the Leg.

    Lift the Leg Straight.Hold the Leg in position for 5/10/15 sec.

  • Back Lift

    Fold with knee. Lift the Back and also opposite. Hold the Back and Leg for 5/10/15 sec.

  • Side and Leg Lift

    Lift with Leg which is towards the ceiling. Hold the Leg in position for 5/10/15 sec.

  • Back Movement of Leg

    Lift the Leg little bit. Take back the Leg and Hold. Hold the Leg in position for 5/10/15 sec.

  • Knee Lift while Sitting.

    Lift the Knee. Hold the Knee in position for 5/10/15 sec. Bring down slowing.

  • Shoulder Excercise

    Rotate the shoulders. slowing for 5/10/15 repeations.

  • Gait Training

    Walkers. slowing for 5/10/15 repeations.
